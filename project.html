Freightr

Freightr is a comprehensive shipment management app tailored for businesses and their customers. It allows businesses to efficiently organize and update shipment details, providing a streamlined interface for tracking shipments. Customers can easily check the status of their shipments, ensuring transparency and convenience. Additionally, the app enables employees to create accounts, offering them secure access to manage shipments, update statuses, and ensure smooth operations. By centralizing shipment data and improving communication, Freightr helps businesses optimize their supply chain and enhance customer satisfaction.

Yatzy Score Tracker

Introducing our Yatzy Score Tracker web application, where users can showcase their top scores and compete for the coveted leaderboard positions. Players simply submit their high scores along with a screenshot for verification, ensuring transparency and fairness. Scores are then prominently displayed on the webpage, ranked from highest to lowest, allowing everyone to track their progress and compare their achievements. In the second phase, administrators can effortlessly manage the scores by verifying screenshots, maintaining the integrity of the leaderboard.
